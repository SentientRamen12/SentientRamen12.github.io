About Me

Hi there! I'm Shubham Rastogi, a passionate software developer with a diverse background, committed to leveraging technology to create impactful solutions. Growing up in Indonesia as an Indian, I developed a unique perspective on how technology can empower communities and drive economic growth. My love for exploring cultures and people through food, architecture, and meaningful interactions has shaped my worldview and approach to problem-solving.

Professional Journey

My professional journey began at Pingal Technologies, where I engineered real-time customer tracking solutions using advanced ML techniques. At Undostres, I developed database and logging layers for a Wallet as a Service product, enabling multi-tenant capabilities. My next significant role was at Ula, a Sequoia-funded startup in Indonesia, where I led a team to build critical social commerce features, enhancing our platform's capabilities and significantly increasing app usage and monthly GMV. Previously, at Wadhwani AI, I lead the development of AI-based solutions for social impact, focusing on assessing and remedying Indic language fluency in public schools across India. This role has allowed me to scale solutions and reduce infrastructure costs while expanding our reach.

In addition to my professional roles, I pursued a B.Tech in Computer Engineering with a minor in Machine Learning and Distributed Systems at Delhi Technological University, followed by an M.S. in Computer Science at New York University, where I continue to deepen my expertise and knowledge.
